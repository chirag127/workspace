# Video hosting services

The family uses a two-provider split so privacy-sensitive video never
touches the YouTube tracking pixel.

| Service | Status | One-line role |
|---|---|---|
| [youtube.md](./youtube.md) | active | Public-content video — unlimited free, accepts tracking trade-off |
| [gumlet.md](./gumlet.md) | active | Privacy-sensitive video — 250 GB/month free, no viewer tracking |
